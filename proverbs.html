Proverbs Reference Library


Proverbs typically refer to short clever sayings that offer some sort of wisdom.
This book has a lot of those. Most of the actual proverbs are in Chapter 10-29.
This section of the books is meant to become a reference work that you return to again and again.


Proverbs 1:1-7:
- for attaining wisdom and discipline.
- for understanding words of insight.
- for acquiring a disciplined and prudent life.
- doing what is right and just and fair.
- for giving prudence to the simple, knowledge and discretion to the young.


The fear of the LORD is the beginning of knowledge, but fools despise wisdom and discipline.
