Poverty

The Lord will not let the godly go hungry, but he refuses to satisfy the craving of the wicked. (10:3)

The wealth of the rich is their forliess; the poverty of the poor is their desliuction. (10:15)

People curse those who hoard their grain, but they bless the one who sells in time of need. (11:26)

Some who are poor pretend to be rich; others who are rich pretend to be poor. (13:7)

The rich can pay a ransom for their lives, but the poor won't even get threatened. (13:8)

If you ignore criticism, you will end in poverty and disgrace; if you accept correction, you will be honored. (13:18)

It is a sin to belittle one's neighbor; blessed are those who help the poor. (14:21)

Work brings profit, but mere talk leads to poverty! (14:23)

Those who oppress the poor insult their Maker, but helping the poor honors him. (14:31)

Better to live humbly with the poor than to share plunder with the proud. (16:19)

Those who mock the poor insult their Maker; those who rejoice at the misfortune of others will be punished. (17:5)

The rich think of their wealth as a strong defense; they imagine it to be a high wall of safety. (18:11)

The poor plead for mercy; the rich answer with insults. (18:23)
