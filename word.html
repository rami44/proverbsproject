Words

Wise words come from the lips of people with understanding, but those lacking sense will be beaten with a rod. (10:13)

Too much talk leads to sin. Be sensible and keep your mouth shut. (10:19)

Some people make cutting remarks, but the words of the wise bring healing. (12:18)

Truthful words stand the test of time, but lies are soon exposed. (12:19)

The Lord detests lying lips, but he delights in those who tell the truth. (12:22)

Even fools are thought wise when they keep silent; with their mouths shut, they seem intelligent. (17:28)

The mouths of fools are their ruin; they trap themselves with their lips. (18:7)

Rumors are dainty morsels that sink deep into one's heart. (18:8)

Spouting off before listening to the facts is both shameful and foolish. (18:13)

Wise words satisfy like a good meal; the right words bring satisfaction. (18:20)
