Women

A gracious woman gains respect, but ruthless men gain only wealth. (11:16)

A beautiful woman who lacks discretion is like a gold ring in a pig's snout. (11:22)

A worthy wife is a crown for her husband, but a disgraceful woman is like cancer in his bones. (12:4)

A wise woman builds her home, but a foolish woman tears it down with her own hands. (14:1)

The man who finds a wife finds a treasure, and he receives favor from the Lord. (18:22)
