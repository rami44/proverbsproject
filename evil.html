Evil

The earnings of the godly enhance their lives, but evil people squander their money on sin. (10:16)

Evil people get rich for the moment, but the reward of the godly will last. (11:18)

Godly people find life; evil people find death. (11:19)

Evil people will surely be punished, but the children of the godly will go free. (11:21)

If you search for good, you will find favor; but if you search for evil, it will find you! (11:27)

Deceit fills hearts that are plotting evil; joy fills hearts that are planning peace! (12:20)

Wise words will win you a good meal, but treacherous people have an appetite for violence. (13:2)

Godliness guards the path of the blameless, but the evil are misled by sin. (13:6)

It is pleasant to see dreams come true, but fools refuse to turn from evil to attain them. (13:19)

Evil people will bow before good people; the wicked will bow at the gates of the godly. (14:19)

If you plan to do evil, you will be lost; if you plan to do good, you will receive unfailing love and faithfulness. (14:22)

The Lord is watching everywhere, keeping his eye on both the evil and the good. (15:3)

The Lord detests evil plans, but he delights in pure words. (15:26)

Unfailing love and faithfulness make atonement for sin. By fearing the Lord, people avoid evil. (16:6)

The path of the virtuous leads away from evil; whoever follows that path is safe. (16:17)

With narrowed eyes, people plot evil; with a smirk, they plan their mischief. (16:30)

Evil people are eager for rebellion, but they will be severely punished. (17:11)

If you repay good with evil, evil will never leave your house. (17:13)

Doing wrong leads to disgrace, and scandalous behavior brings contempt. (18:3)
